About Me

From an early age on, I found great pleasure in drawing and painting. At some point, I realised that I wanted to pursue this path professionally. While searching online, I discovered the profession of the scenic artist, and an internship at the Heidelberg Theatre confirmed that this could be the right direction for me.
I then completed a three-year apprenticeship in the scenic workshop, where I learned to work professionally with paint, brushes and a variety of materials, acquiring the full range of scenic painting techniques.
By the end of this period, I began to wonder whether I wanted to deepen my skills. The wish to refine my painting technique, led me to the Academy of Fine Arts in Dresden. There, I found more freedom for experimentation and gained new artistic perspectives.
Since then, I have worked at various theatres, alongside different colleagues and within diverse methods. Each position has contributed to my professional development.
Here, you can find an overview of my professional journey so far—from shorter engagements to longer-term positions.
