'Sleeping Beauty'

For the ballet production Sleeping Beauty, a collaboration between Theatre Erfurt and Theatre Altenburg Gera, I painted various scenic elements, including the backdrop, side wings, and the main valance. The large-scale palace backdrop forms the central visual element of the stage design. In addition, the side wings and the main valance were executed in a consistent visual style to create a coherent and spatially unified stage environment. All elements are based on the set design by Timur Kinzikeev and were developed through architectural research and translated into both drawing and painting. This resulted in a convincing illusion of space, supporting the baroque atmosphere of the production visually.
