Graduation Project

As part of my diploma project, I created a scenic painting interpretation of a photograph of an abandoned theatre in Belgium. The motif of this decaying architecture was developed as a large-scale backdrop (6 × 9 m). The work translates a photographic reference into a painted stage setting, combining composition, perspective, and colour design to create an illusion of spatial depth.
