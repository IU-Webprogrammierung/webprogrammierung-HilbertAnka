The Profession Of Scenic Artistry

On this page, you can learn more about the fascinating and traditional craft of scenic painting — an art form that has changed very little over the many centuries of practice. You can also explore a selection of works I have created over the past years for various stage productions.

Scenery Painting

Have you ever wondered who builds and paints the stage sets for theatre productions? Behind every larger theatre is a team of skilled craftspeople working together to bring productions to life. One of them is the scenic artist, working in the scenic studio. Using a theatre backdrop as an example, I’ll show you how such large-scale paintings are created and what this craft can look like in practice.
